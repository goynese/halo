About This Collection

The Project

The Halo: Combat Evolved Short Stories collection is a fan fiction project dedicated to celebrating the iconic moments, characters, and settings from Bungie's groundbreaking 2001 first-person shooter. Each story in this collection explores a different perspective or key moment from the game, offering readers a chance to experience the Halo universe through new eyes.

This collection was created out of love for the rich storytelling and immersive world of the Halo franchise. The goal was to capture the essence of what made the original game so memorable while adding depth to characters and moments that players experienced during their journey through Installation 04.

The Stories

This collection features ten short stories, each focusing on a different aspect of the Halo: Combat Evolved narrative:

  • First Encounter - Master Chief's initial battle with the Covenant aboard the Pillar of Autumn
  • Beach Assault - The iconic Silent Cartographer beach landing
  • Flood Reveal - The terrifying first encounter with the parasitic Flood
  • Race Against Time - The final Warthog escape sequence
  • The Oracle's Duty - 343 Guilty Spark's perspective on events
  • Honor and Heresy - A Covenant Elite's experience during the conflict
  • Digital Sentinel - Cortana's unique AI perspective
  • The Captain's Last Stand - Captain Keyes' leadership and sacrifice
  • The Sergeant's Speech - Sergeant Johnson rallying his Marines
  • Just Another Day in Hell - A regular UNSC Marine's perspective

The Music

This website features a selection of iconic music from the Halo: Combat Evolved soundtrack, composed by Martin O'Donnell and Michael Salvatori. The soundtrack is widely regarded as one of the most memorable and influential video game scores ever created, with its distinctive blend of orchestral arrangements, ancient chants, and electronic elements.

The audio player allows you to enjoy these classic tracks while reading the stories, enhancing the immersive experience of the Halo universe.
